<span style="font-weight: 900;">New Publication</span>: Our latest [study](http://dx.doi.org/10.1029/2025GL119841) on the global evolution of atmospheric rivers is now published in the *Geophysical Research Letters*. Utilizing the <span style="font-weight: 900;">Vapor Kinetic Energy (VKE)</span> framework, we demonstrate that atmospheric rivers are fundamentally driven by the conversion of potential energy (PE) to kinetic energy (KE) across all ocean basins, providing a unified energetic perspective on their intensification.
