Our recent [study](https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1029/2025GL119841) uses the Vapor Kinetic Energy (VKE) framework to show that ARs follow a similar evolution path globally, with some regional variation. Their growth is primarily driven by PE-to-KE conversion, and their movement is associated with the horizontal convergence of VKE flux.
